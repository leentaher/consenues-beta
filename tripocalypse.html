crisis 01

AI is eating jobs.

Your job is probably fine. For now.

If you do your job on a computer, AI will soon be able to do it too. And yes, it will create whole new industries. New enterprises. New opportunities.

AI will staff those too.

By 2029, whoever is president will inherit an economy where the professional-managerial class is one missed payment away from mortgage default. The working class was already there. Now everyone else joins them.

crisis 02

Climate is eating continents.

Your house is probably fine. For now.

Miami is underwater. Phoenix hits 120° in June and stays there. Wildfires are burning through towns that have never burned before. The insurance companies already ran the numbers - that's why they're leaving. That's why your premiums doubled. That's why "act of God" is being quietly redefined to mean "not our problem."

The physics is simple and merciless: the warmer it gets, the more ice melts. The more ice melts, the warmer it gets. There's no thermostat. There's no off switch. There's just the bill, which arrives every year and gets larger.

Every year we wait, the cost of fixing it doubles. We've been waiting for fifty years.

crisis 03

Fascism is eating democracy.

It had a lot of different names before it got to this one.

It introduced itself as "free speech." As "common sense." As "just asking questions." Then, without a single turning point, we got to mass deportations. Masked federal agents in unmarked vans. Unarmed civilians shot. Entire articles of the constitution quietly set aside.

By the time it's obvious, it's usually too late. Next stop: canceled elections. Martial law.

Economic desperation is the fuel. Scapegoats are the match. Both are currently in record supply.
