crisis 01

AI is eating jobs

Your job is probably fine. For now.

AI isn't replacing jobs all at once. It's eating them category by category. First the routine stuff. Then the creative stuff. Then the stuff we thought was safe.

The new industries AI creates? They'll be staffed by AI. Not by you.

By 2029, whoever is president will inherit an economy where a significant chunk of the professional class can't make rent. The working class was already there. Now everyone else joins them.

crisis 02

Climate is eating cities

Not a future problem. A now problem.

Miami is flooding. Phoenix is on fire. The insurance companies already know — they're leaving. The question isn't if. It's how fast.

Every year we wait, the cost of fixing it doubles.

crisis 03

Fascism is eating democracy

It doesn't announce itself. It shows up as "efficiency." As "common sense." As "just asking questions."

By the time it's obvious, it's usually too late. The data already shows where we are.

Economic desperation is the fuel. Scapegoats are the match. Both are in abundant supply.
