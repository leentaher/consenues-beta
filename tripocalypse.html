02 CRISIS TWO

Climate is eating
continents.

Your house is probably fine. For now.

Miami is underwater. Phoenix hits 120 degrees in June and stays there. Wildfires are burning through towns that have never burned before. The insurance companies already ran the numbers — that's why they're leaving.

The physics is simple and merciless: the warmer it gets, the more ice melts. The more ice melts, the warmer it gets. There's no thermostat. There's no off switch. There's just the bill, which arrives every year and gets larger.

03 CRISIS THREE

Fascism is eating
democracy.

It had a lot of different names before it got to this one.

It introduced itself as "free speech." As "common sense." As "just asking questions." Then, without a single turning point, we got to mass deportations. Masked federal agents. Unarmed civilians shot. By the time it's obvious, it's usually too late.

Economic desperation is the fuel. Scapegoats are the match. Both are currently in record supply.
